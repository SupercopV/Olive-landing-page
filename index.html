Everything you need to know about Olive

What does Olive's food score mean? ▾

Olive scores products from 0–100 based on ingredient quality, additive safety, and nutritional value. A score above 70 is considered good, 50–70 average, and below 50 means the product contains ingredients you may want to avoid for your family.

How accurate is the ingredient database? ▾

Our database covers over 2 million products and is updated daily. We source data from manufacturers, public databases, and a network of nutrition researchers who verify ingredient information regularly.

Can I use Olive for my child's dietary restrictions? ▾

Absolutely. Olive lets you set custom filters for common allergens (nuts, dairy, gluten, soy) and over 50 dietary preferences. Products that trigger your filters are clearly flagged during scanning.

Is my data private with Olive? ▾

Your health data and scan history are encrypted and never sold to third parties. We are 100% independent and earn revenue only from subscriptions, not advertising or brand partnerships.

Does Olive work for restaurant menus? ▾

Yes! Olive has a growing restaurant database that covers major chains and independent restaurants. You can check ingredient transparency and allergen information before ordering.
